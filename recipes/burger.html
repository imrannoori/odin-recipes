Ingrediants

Steps

  1. Gather all necessary ingredients and equipment.
  2. Form the ground beef into patties.
  3. Heat a skillet or grill over medium-high heat.
  4. Cook the patties for 3-4 minutes per side, or until desired doneness is reached.
  5. Place the cooked patty on a bun and add desired toppings.
  6. Serve and enjoy!
