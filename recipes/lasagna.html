Lasagna

Description

Alright, dah-ling, let me tell you about this lasagna that's gonna blow your mind!

Ingrediants

  • Homemade marinara sauce (tomatoes, onions, garlic, olive oil, salt, pepper, sugar, herbs)
  • Lasagna pasta
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Ground beef or sausage
  • Nutmeg
  • Fresh basil

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit (190 degrees Celsius).
  2. Cook lasagna pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large pan, brown the ground beef or sausage over medium heat. Drain any excess fat.
  4. Mix together the ricotta, mozzarella, parmesan, nutmeg and some chopped basil in a bowl.
  5. In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of marinara sauce on the bottom.
  6. Arrange a layer of cooked lasagna pasta on top of the marinara sauce.
  7. Spread a layer of the cheese mixture on top of the pasta.
  8. Add a layer of the meat, then another layer of marinara sauce.
  9. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, ending with a layer of marinara sauce and mozzarella cheese on top.
  10. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  11. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes or until the cheese is golden brown and bubbly.
  12. Remove from oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving.
