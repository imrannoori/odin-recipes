Meatballs

DESCRIPTION

Alright, dah-ling, let me tell you about this meatball dish that's gonna knock your taste buds right outta the park!

Ingrediants

  • 1 pound of premium beef
  • Chopped parsley
  • Grated Parmesan cheese
  • Secret blend of spices
  • Homemade marinara sauce (tomatoes, garlic, onions)
  • Creamy, garlic mashed potatoes
  • Fresh chopped basil
  • Extra virgin olive oil

Steps

  1. Mix the beef, parsley, Parmesan cheese, and secret blend of spices together in a large bowl.
  2. Form the mixture into evenly sized meatballs.
  3. Sear the meatballs in a pan or grill to brown the exterior.
  4. Add the meatballs to a pot with the homemade marinara sauce and simmer for about 20-25 minutes or until cooked through.
  5. Prepare the creamy, garlic mashed potatoes according to your recipe.
  6. Serve the meatballs over the mashed potatoes and garnish with fresh chopped basil and a drizzle of extra virgin olive oil.
